ArttulOS Privacy Policy

We do not collect, track, or store any of your personal information — ever.

No Telemetry

ArttulOS does not include telemetry, tracking, or background data reporting tools of any kind. We respect your right to privacy entirely.

Local-Only Software

All software in ArttulOS runs entirely on your own machine. No part of the system phones home or connects to analytics services.

Open Source by Design

All our code is open source under the AGPL v3.0 license. You can inspect it yourself to verify that your privacy is never violated.

No Account, No Problem

ArttulOS does not require an account, registration, or activation key. You use it completely anonymously.
