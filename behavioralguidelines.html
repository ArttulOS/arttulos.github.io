ArttulOS Behavior Guidelines

ArttulOS is a community dedicated to building great software. We respect all religions, political beliefs, and backgrounds.

Respect and Collaboration

We ask everyone to focus on the software, not on personal beliefs or politics. All discussions should be centered around collaboration, innovation, and respect.

Zero Tolerance for Political & Religious Debate

Political and religious discussions are not allowed in any official channels or contributions. This is to keep our environment welcoming, focused, and free of conflict.

Everyone is Welcome

No matter your background, faith, or views — if you respect this policy and contribute positively, you have a place here.
